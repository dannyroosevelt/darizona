📋 How it all works
⛳ Masters Pool
🏎Pick one golfer per tier (6 picks total). Each tier is priced by Vegas odds — Tier 1 is the favorites, Tier 6 is the legends.
🎯Scoring: Each golfer's finishing position counts. Add up all 6 positions — lowest total wins.
✂Missed cut = +80 penalty per golfer. Early withdrawal (WD) same penalty.
💰Payout: 70% to 1st place, 30% to 2nd place.
🔒Picks lock Thursday 8am ET when the tournament begins. No changes after that.
🎲 Saturday Prop Bets
✍10 questions about the Saturday round at McDowell Mountain. Lock in your answers before tee-off.
🎯Scoring: Most correct answers wins the whole prop pot. For number guesses (Darren's score, etc.) closest answer counts as correct — tie goes to the lower guess.
💰Payout: 1st place (most points) takes 90%, 2nd takes 10%. Tiebreak: closest guess on Darren's exact score. If still tied, lower guess wins.
✍Commissioner enters results after the round — the app calculates payouts automatically.
🤳 The one rule
🏑Darren gets no gimmies. Every putt counts. Ask for one inside 3 feet — it's denied and he drinks.
