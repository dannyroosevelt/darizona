📋 How it all works
⛳ Masters Pool
🏎Pick one golfer per tier (6 picks total). Each tier is priced by Vegas odds — Tier 1 is the favorites, Tier 6 is the legends.

💰Payout: 70% to 1st place, 30% to 2nd place.
